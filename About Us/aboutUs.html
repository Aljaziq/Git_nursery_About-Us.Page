About Us

A specialized nursery to take care of children from birth to pre-school, we provide them with confidence and surround them with care, in an encouraging environment under distinguished supervision.

Our Goals

  • Providing a safe, healthy and stimulating educational environment for the child's development near his mother.
  • The comprehensive and integrated development of the child in all educational, psychological, health, social and behavioral fields.
  • Providing the child with life skills by accustoming him to taking responsibility and assessing his needs.
  • Develop different physical senses according to the child's natural growth stages.
  • Preparing a child who is able to achieve community participation to be an active member in his community.
  • Providing attractive and purposeful activities to teach the child research and exploration.
  • Develop the child's ability to express and communicate.
  • Consolidation of religious, national and moral values.
  • Qualifying the child for formal education and his transition to school life by providing him with basic concepts in Islamic education, Arabic language, mathematics and English.


Our Features

  • Suitable working times.
  • Long experience for incubators and teachers.
  • Nursing staff with high qualifications.
  • Enrichment and educational programs.

  • The Guidelines:
  • Show the card when entering the security and reveal the face upon entring.
  • The working hours start at seven o’clock in the morning and it is forbidden to enter the building before the start of work, and work ends at five o’clock in the evening. It is forbidden to stay in the building after the end of working hours.
  • No visitors or escorts are allowed to enter .
  • Obligation to implement the preliminary examination procedures at the inspection point.
  • The child must be handed over and received by the child's mother or someone authorized by her with official consent.The person who authorized must signature on the custom record designated for this.
  • Please wait in the designated places and do not walk around the nursery facilities.




    • The Health Care

    • Health Care:
    • Nurses permanently residing in the nursery.
    • The nurse takes the temperature of the children daily at the beginning of the day.
    • The nurse makes periodic visits to the children to ensure that the hair is clean and that the child is free of infectious diseases.
    • If the child’s temperature rises while he is in the nursery, or he shows symptoms of influenza, or if he suffers from vomiting or diarrhea, the nurse contacts the mother to receive the child.
    • In the event that the child, -God forbid-, is exposed to any emergency while he is in the nursery, the nurse performs first aid and directs the case.
    • The nursery is obligated not to give the child any medication without the mother’s written consent, and in cases of long-term treatment, the nursery is obligated to provide the medically prescribed medication and based on the mother’s written recommendations.
    • Nurses and teachers receive health conditions for each registered child.
    • Health Care:
    • The nursery has special rooms for breastfeeding.
    • The nursery encourages children to eat healthy, nutritious foods.
    • It is strictly forbidden to bring unhealthy foods such as: soda, chips and sugars.
    • Enrichment and educational programs.


    • Our Care Of The Cleanliness:
    • Cleaning the entire building twice a day by the cleaning staff and under the supervision of the competent department.
    • The bathrooms are cleaned continuously throughout the day.
    • Dispose of the waste immediately and put it in the trash.
    • Sterilization of the entire building every two hours by the sterilization employee and under the supervision of the nurse.
    • Spraying the building for flying insects periodically by the university administration.
